MY JOURNEY

Computer Science Bachelor focused on digital imaging (2022-2025)

After my baccalaureate, I needed to find studies that matched my future profession. I then discovered the technological universitary institute of Puy-en-Velay as being the only establishment in France to offer publicly a computer science bachelor focused on digital imaging. You can learn to program on highly visual and graphic projects, and there are even 3D modeling classes ! So, without further ado, I was off to Puy-en-Velay after being quickly accepted for this course.

IT Baccalaureate with Numeric Innovation & Sciences option (obtained in 2022)

In my last year at middle school, I knew what I wanted to do: develop video games. I was already seeing code as the skeleton of the game. It's what gives life, motion and brings together all the other elements a game needs. That's why I chose a course of study that would get me closer and closer to this path. I passed my baccalaureate in 2022 with honors, continuing to pursue my ambitions.
